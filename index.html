About

I am a UX Designer based in Chicago, IL. I am a recent transplant from Denver, CO. I believe that data-driven design removes much inefficient subjectivity and allows products to be molded for the most important people in the conversation - the users.

I began my journey studying psychology as an undergrad. I was and still am fascinated by the complexities of human behavior and wanted to gain a deeper understanding of what motivates people. After graduation, I found another passion in programming. Ilove language and problem solving, and I felt a powerful draw to the tech world. I havespent the last few years working professionally as a web applications developer.

Now I feel called again to the fascinating world of UX - a field that sits beautifully between psychology and development. I have completed an immersive UX course, which has equipped me with a broad understanding of a UX professional’s role. I have a strong interest usability testing, interaction design and, increasingly, visual design.

I can bring to your team coded wireframes and prototypes in addition tousing standard design software such as Invision and Photoshop. I know how to design forboth business and user needs and make sure those designs are technically feasible. And, of course, I bring endless curiosity for the craft of UX. Let’s work and learn together! Contact me.
