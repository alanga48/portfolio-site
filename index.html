About Me

I believe that data-driven design removes much inefficient subjectivity and allows products to be molded for the most important people in the conversation - the users.

I began my journey studying psychology as an undergrad. I was and still am fascinated by the complexities of human behavior and wanted to gain a deeper understanding of what motivates people. After graduation, I found another passion in programming and worked as a web applications developer for a few years.

Now I feel called again to the fascinating world of UX - a field that sits beautifully between psychology and development. I can bring to your team coded wireframes and prototypes in addition to utilizing standard design software. I know how to design for both business and user needs and make sure those designs are technically feasible. I am passionate about understanding people and creating products made especially for them.

Let's work and learn together! Contact me.
